Jose's Legendary Chocolate Chip Cookies 🍪✨

Welcome, cookie lovers! You’re about to unlock the secrets of the *most legendary* chocolate chip cookies around. Proceed with caution, they might just become your favorite snack. 😉

✨ Magic Ingredients ✨

  • 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour (measured with love)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda (the secret to fluffiness!)
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter (soften it up, don’t rush it)

🚀 Instructions to Cookie Greatness 🚀

  1. Preheat that oven to 350°F (175°C) - let’s warm things up! 🔥
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda—be the master of your cookie magic potion. 🪄
  3. Mix everything else, throw in those chocolate chips, and get ready for some dough-licious action!
  4. Eat a few spoonfuls (I won't judge) and then make your cookie scoops for the baking sheet. 🍪
  5. Bake until golden, and wait for the magic to happen... or at least until you can smell that cookie goodness! 🤤
